The Truth Foundation

Break free, live fully.

The Truth Foundation is a counselling-focused nonprofit organization dedicated to helping youths and young adults overcome addiction, rediscover purpose, and build healthier and more fulfilling lives through professional guidance and support.

About Us

The Truth Foundation is dedicated to empowering communities through education, advocacy, and action. We believe in fostering positive change by providing resources, support, and opportunities for individuals and groups to thrive. Our mission is to promote truth, justice, and equality in all aspects of life, ensuring that everyone has access to the tools and knowledge needed to build a better future.
Through our programs, we focus on education initiatives, community outreach, and advocacy for social issues. We collaborate with partners to deliver impactful solutions that address real-world challenges and create lasting positive effects.
Our foundation is built on the principles of integrity, compassion, and innovation, driving us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, advocacy, and action, promoting truth, justice, and equality for a better future.

Our Impact

Our impact is centered on transforming lives through counselling, early intervention, and sustained support. We measure success by recovery progress, improved well-being, and positive life outcomes among the youths and young adults we serve.

Our Counselling Programs

Youth Addiction Counselling

Professional one-on-one counselling tailored to youths struggling with substance and behavioral addictions. Our approach focuses on recovery, emotional healing, and long-term personal growth.

Young Adult Recovery Support

Structured counselling support for young adults seeking to overcome addiction, rebuild confidence, and develop healthy coping strategies for everyday life.

Online and Physical Counselling Sessions

Flexible counselling services offered both in-person and online, ensuring access to professional support regardless of location.

Addiction Awareness and Prevention

Educational initiatives focused on early intervention, awareness, and prevention of addiction among youths and young adults.

Leadership and Professional Background

The Truth Foundation is led by trained counselling professionals committed to ethical practice, confidentiality, and youth development.

• Bachelor’s Degree in Guidance and Counselling

• Ongoing professional certifications

• Strong commitment to ethical counselling standards and confidentiality

Counselling Resources

The Truth Foundation provides a variety of counselling resources to support mental health and addiction recovery. Our counselling guides, recovery worksheets, mental health articles, and support group information are designed to foster healing and positive change. These materials are available for download and use by individuals seeking support.

Counselling Guides

Comprehensive guides on various counselling topics, including addiction recovery, mental health support, and personal development.

Recovery Worksheets
